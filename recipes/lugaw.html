Lugaw

Description

You probably know what I'm already gonna say, but I'll say it again anyway. Lugaw is yet another one of my favorite Filipino dishes from my childhood. It's porridge, but with some extra spices.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup long grain white rice
  • 4-5 cups water
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 cup diced chicken breast
  • 4 cloves garlic diced
  • 2 tbsp diced ginger

Steps

  1. Add 1tbsp oil to pan and let heat.
  2. Sautee garlic and ginger for a minute
  3. Add chicken, and fry until brown
  4. Take out chicken and spices.
  5. Pour water in a cooking pot. Bring to a boil.
  6. Put-in the rice. Continue cooking for 30 minutes or until the texture becomes thick, while stirring once in awhile.
  7. Add the salt, readd chicken and spices, then stir and then cook for 2 minutes more.
  8. Transfer to a serving bowl. Top with a tablespoon of rousing.
  9. Serve hot. Share and enjoy!
